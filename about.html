🛡️ About Free Strong Password Generator

Plain English password security. No jargon. No complexity. Just tools that work.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe strong password security shouldn't require a computer science degree. Our mission is simple: make creating secure passwords as easy as making a cup of tea. Every tool on this site is designed with beginners in mind — no technical knowledge needed, just follow the wizard.

"Password security is for everyone. If you can read this, you can create a password that would take a hacker centuries to crack."

🔒 How We Keep You Safe

🏠100% Client-Side

All passwords are generated in your browser using CSPRNG. Nothing ever leaves your device — we never see, store, or transmit your passwords.

📋NIST-Aligned

Our generation follows NIST SP 800-63B 2025 guidelines and NCSC recommendations. Enterprise-grade standards, made simple.

🔓Zero Logging

We don't log, track, or analyse anything you do on our password tools. Complete privacy by design.

🌍Free Forever

No paywalls, no premium tiers, no account required. Strong passwords should be free for everyone, everywhere.

📖 Why We Built This

Most password generators online are either too technical, too sketchy, or too complicated for everyday people. We saw family members struggling with "uppercase, lowercase, special character" requirements and decided to build something better.

FreeStrongPassword.com is the result — a tool that explains password strength in plain English, walks you through creating one step-by-step, and never judges you for reusing that one password you've had since 2015. (But seriously, use a different one for each site.)
