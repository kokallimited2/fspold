Cookie Policy

Here's exactly what we store and why.

🌿 Short version: We use almost no cookies. The only thing we save is whether you clicked "OK" on the cookie banner — and that stays in your browser, never sent to us.

What We Store

NameWhat it doesHow long
fsp-ckRemembers if you've accepted our cookie notice. Stays in your browser only — never sent to us.Until you clear your browser data

How to Clear Them

To remove the cookie notice preference: open your browser's developer tools → Application → Local Storage → delete fsp-ck.

To clear all browser storage: go to your browser's settings and look for "Clear browsing data" or "Privacy and security".
