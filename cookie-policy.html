Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and how to manage them — explained simply.

Last updated: 6 May 2026

Good news: We use very few cookies — far fewer than most websites. Our password generator needs no cookies at all. This page tells you exactly what we do use and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored in your browser when you visit a website. We also use localStorage — browser storage that works similarly but never sends data to any server.

How to Delete Cookies

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search and services → Clear browsing data

To clear our localStorage entry: open Developer Tools (F12) → Application → Local Storage → freestrongpassword.com → delete fsp-ck. The consent banner will reappear on your next visit.
