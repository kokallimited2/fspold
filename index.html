Security experts recommend at least 16 characters for everyday accounts, and 20+ for important accounts like email and banking. Our wizard defaults to 20 — which is excellent for almost everyone.
They do help make it stronger, but length matters more. A 20-character password with just letters is safer than an 8-character one with lots of symbols. Use all four types if you can — our wizard makes it easy.
Modern security advice says you should only change your password if you think it's been stolen, or if a website you use tells you they've been hacked. Changing it just because time has passed isn't needed.
